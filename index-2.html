$TACKIN

$TACKIN, also known as Chris Amparo, is a young Dominican-American songwriter, musician, and artist. $TACKIN is known for his poetic lyrics and original tune. Growing up in Montgomery County, MD he has had the best opportunities to master his craft in the diverse area. $TACKIN looks for the best intersection of culture, lyrics, and emotion. Music has always been a passion to him. Whether it was beachside concerts in Santo Domingo or house parties, $TACKIN was found at the center of the music and dance. $TACKIN has many musical influences and is excited for the upcoming releases of his legendary music.
