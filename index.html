About Us

Sumptuous hot breakfasts and hearty lunches make Bandannas a favorite among hotel guests and Park City locals. Start your day with all your breakfast favorites or enjoy a relaxing afternoon with a thick, juicy burger or stacked sandwich. Enjoy a beautiful setting with your choice of lobster, salmon, artisan breads, beef sliders, and fresh locally grown vegetables.

For a great breakfast or lunch in Park City, try the Bandannas Bar & Grill. We serve a variety of American favorites, including distinctive burgers. Located in beautiful Park City, Utah at the Hotel Park City, we're ready to serve you. Come in today!
